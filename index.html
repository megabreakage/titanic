Titanic Resort

Ruiru

your ultimate Turn Up Joint

Titanic Resort-Ruiru, also known as “Kariaini” meaning “by the lake side” is an upcoming establishment located at Ruiru in Central Kenya. It’s a serene joint that offers a cocktail of services from Bar and Grill, Car-Wash, Boat ridding and many more

It’s strategic location away from the hussle and bussle of city life will make u feel welcomed following the friendly and helpful staff, good food & drinks and to top it up…the cool day breeze from the reservoir.

Titanic Resort-Ruiru, Bar, Mini-Restaurant and the lawns brings to being every customer’s desire and feeling of hospitality and comfort-ability translating every customer’s imaginations and dreams into reality.

It engraves a sense of class and touch into the customer’s hearts as they get to experience the homely services offered in addition to the serene environment and surroundings which are characterized by fresh air and the refreshing gentle breeze that blows from the shows of the dam.

Titanic Resort-Ruiru, is all you can drink and eat kind of bar and restaurant, serving most brands of locally and internationally approved drinks and Barbeque prepared in the local style.

Traditional drinks are also available on advanced request

Enjoy several juicy cuts of charcoal grilled goat meat, beef, pork, mutton, chicken or fish.

The choice of preparation is entirely based on your taste and preferences.
