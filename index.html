ABOUT US

Titanic also known as “Kariaini” meaning “by the lake side” is an upcoming establishment located at Ruiru in Central Kenya. It’s a serene joint that offers a cocktail of services from Bar and Grill, Car-Wash, Boat ridding and many more

It’s strategic location away from the hussle and bussle of city life will make u feel welcomed following the friendly and helpful staff, good food & drinks and to top it up…the cool day breeze from the reservoir.

Titanic bar and Mini-Restaurant brings to being every customer’s desire and feeling of hospitality comfort-ability translating every customer’s imaginations and dreams into reality.

It engraves a sense of class and touch into the customer’s hearts as they get to experience the homely services offered in addition to the serene environment and surroundings which are characterized by fresh air and the refreshing gentle breeze that blows from the shows of the dam.
